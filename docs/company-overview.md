# NexAI Labs — Company Overview

## Products & Services

### Studio Vision AI (AI Photoshoots)

Our most mature product. Transforms mannequin/flatlay images into studio-quality on-model photography using AI.

**Value prop:** Replace traditional photoshoots (expensive, slow, logistically complex) with instant AI-generated images at a fraction of the cost.

**Key metrics:**

- 10,000+ images generated
- 25+ brands served
- 80% time saved vs traditional shoots
- Up to 90% cost reduction

**Services:**

- On-model photos from mannequin/flatlay shots
- Lifestyle backgrounds for campaigns
- Product page optimizations for marketplaces (Myntra, Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify)
- Campaign & social media content
- Seasonal collection drops

**Clients:** BannoSwagger, Style Fables, Jugo Superfoods, Saree Mall, IndoEra, Dhwani Bansal Jewelry, and others.

### Miles (AI Sales Agent)

AI-powered SDR (Sales Development Representative) that handles outbound sales automation.

**Capabilities:**

- LinkedIn enrichment + outreach
- AI phone calling agent
- Post-call analysis (sentiment, CRM notes, calendar scheduling)
- Automated follow-up sequences

### Other Services

- **Workflow Automation** — connecting apps and eliminating manual processes
- **Content Automation** — AI-generated blogs, emails, social posts matching brand voice
- **Support Automation** — chatbots that solve problems and escalate when needed

## Current Strategic Context

The website is being rebuilt from a Framer-based site to a custom Astro 5 build. Key strategic considerations:

1. **Brand unification needed** — Studio Vision AI and NexAI Labs currently feel like separate brands
2. **AI Photoshoots is the flagship** — most mature product with clearest value prop and pricing
3. **Target market:** Indian D2C fashion and e-commerce brands
4. **Competitive landscape:** AI photoshoot space is growing (Caimera.ai, Botika, Photoroom, etc.)
5. **Differentiation:** Speed (4-day deployment), outcome-based ROI, full-stack offering
