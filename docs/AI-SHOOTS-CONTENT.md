## Section 9: FAQ

1. **"Do the AI-generated images look realistic?"**
   Yes. Our AI creates photorealistic, studio-quality images that meet marketplace standards (Myntra, Amazon, Flipkart). Most customers cannot distinguish them from traditional photoshoots.

2. **"What images do I need to provide?"**
   Mannequin shots, ghost mannequin, or flat-lay photos. Smartphone quality is perfectly fine — we handle all the AI processing.

3. **"Can I choose the model's look?"**
   Absolutely. Select body type, skin tone, height, poses, expressions, and backgrounds. We can match any brand aesthetic.

4. **"How fast is delivery?"**
   Standard delivery is 48 hours. Growth and Scale plans include priority 24-hour delivery. Rush same-day options available.

5. **"Do I own the images?"**
   100% yes. Once delivered, the images are fully yours to use anywhere — marketplaces, website, social media, ads, print.

6. **"What if I don't like the results?"**
   We offer revision rounds on all plans. If you're still not satisfied, we'll refund your order. We've never had a refund request.

7. **"Is there a minimum order?"**
   Yes, minimum 25 SKUs for pay-per-SKU orders. Monthly plans have no per-order minimum.
